Scan QR code from image

Upload or paste a QR image and inspect the result.

LinkLens can scan QR codes from browser-decodable image files and pasted screenshots. It is designed for quick checks when a QR code appears inside a photo, receipt, poster, document screenshot, or saved image.

If the QR code points to a URL, LinkLens runs a URL reliability check and shows redirects and warning signals before you choose whether to open the destination.
