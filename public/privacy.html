Privacy

Browser-first QR scanning.

LinkLens processes QR images in your browser when possible. Upload, paste, camera, crop, and save workflows are designed to keep image handling local unless a URL reliability check is requested.

URL checks

When a QR code contains a URL, LinkLens sends that URL to the backend to normalize it, resolve redirects, and calculate deterministic risk signals. API keys and optional provider secrets stay server-side.

Limitations

LinkLens reduces risk by showing destination details, signals, limitations, and optional AI explanations. It cannot guarantee that any destination is trustworthy.
