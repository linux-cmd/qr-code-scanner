Privacy Policy

This scanner processes QR images in your browser when possible. URL intelligence requests are sent to the site backend to normalize the URL, resolve redirects, and calculate deterministic risk signals.

API keys are kept server-side. Cache keys should be hashed where possible, and optional third-party threat providers are disabled unless explicitly configured.

The scanner does not guarantee that any destination is safe. It is intended to reduce risk by showing destination, signals, limitations, and optional AI explanations.
