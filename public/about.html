About LinkLens

A scanner that cares where QR links go.

LinkLens is a browser-first QR code scanner for images, screenshots, camera scans, QR crop export, and QR code URL checks.

It is built by Abhijay Panwar, a student developer based in Texas, as a practical tool for checking a QR code link before deciding whether to open it.
