Crop QR code

Extract a QR code from a larger photo.

When LinkLens detects a QR code, it creates a padded crop from the original image and offers it as a PNG download. This helps isolate QR codes from menus, packaging, posters, screenshots, and printed material.

The crop feature works alongside QR decoding, so you can save the visible code and still review the decoded link or text payload.
