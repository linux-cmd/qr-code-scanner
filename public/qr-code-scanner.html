Free QR code scanner

Scan QR codes from photos, screenshots, and your camera.

LinkLens decodes QR codes from practical browser inputs: upload, drag and drop, paste, camera, and manual URL entry. When a URL is found, LinkLens shows the original QR URL and the final destination after redirects.

The scanner also creates a QR crop you can save as a PNG, which is useful when a code is embedded inside a larger poster, cup, flyer, or screenshot.
