Check where a QR code link goes.

QR codes can hide long URLs and redirects. LinkLens shows the original QR URL, the final destination after redirects, the destination domain, and transparent reason bullets for the risk estimate.

The result is not a guarantee. LinkLens avoids saying a site is safe and instead explains what was checked, what was found, and what limitations still apply.
